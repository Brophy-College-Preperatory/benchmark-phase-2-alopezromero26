    There are many famous players and I will include one per league who I think is the face of that League. EPL: Erling Haaland (Manchester City) – Dominates with record-breaking goals and physical prowess. La Liga: Jude Bellingham (Real Madrid) – Transforms midfield with goals and creativity. Serie A: Victor Osimhen (Napoli) – Key to Napoli’s first title in 33 years, a lethal striker. Bundesliga: Jamal Musiala (Bayern Munich) – Young playmaker, defining Bayern’s creative edge. Ligue 1: Kylian Mbappé (PSG) – Global superstar, the face of French football.
