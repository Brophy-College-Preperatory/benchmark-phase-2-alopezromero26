    • Introduction

    Soccer started dating back 2000 B.C in several nations such as Rome, Greek, and China. The ball was usually made out of rock or animal hide stuffed with hair. The "first football association" was created in England in 1863. Rules were created. but rules would change over time. Of course, you could not use your hands and pick up the ball or else it would be a foul. Football gained rapid attention and official matches were held as well as public schools having teams and playing against each other. The growth of soccer made a pathway of it becoming into the most popular sport today.
